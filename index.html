JOCELYN'S GARLIC BUTTER SHRIMP RECIPE

Full Ingredient list:

  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 Â½ pounds medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper (to taste)
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 cup chicken stock
  • 3 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

Instructions:

  1. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large cast iron skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Add shrimp, salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. Cook, stirring occasionally, until pink, about 2-3 minutes; set aside.
  4. Stir in garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Stir in chicken stock and lemon juice.
  6. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until reduced by half, about 1-2 minutes.
  7. Stir in remaining 6 tablespoons butter, 1 tablespoon at a time, until melted and smooth.
  8. Stir in shrimp and gently toss to combine.
  9. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley leaves, if desired.
